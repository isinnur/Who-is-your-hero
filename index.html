Who Is Your Hero

Deadpool

The character is known as the "Merc with a Mouth" because of his tendency to talk and joke constantly, including breaking the fourth wall for humorous effect and running gags.

Spiderman

Spider-Man gets superhuman spider-powers and abilities from a bite from a radioactive spider.

Iron Man

Philanthropist. Tony Stark's confidence is only matched by his high-flying abilities as the hero called Iron Man. Inventor Tony Stark applies his genius for high-tech solutions to problems as Iron Man, the armored Avenger.
